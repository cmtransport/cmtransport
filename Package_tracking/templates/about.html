About Us

When it comes to the transportation of your goods and materials, you can’t afford to trust just anyone. You need to be assured your items will arrive at their destination in the promised timeframe and condition. Our logistics company, headquartered in Painesville, Ohio, and serving nationwide, is dedicated to providing clients with premium white glove service and immediate, same-day door-to-door expedited services, 24 hours a day, 365 days per year.
At C&M Transport, our team of professionals offers superior transportation services to customers nationwide, and we pride ourselves on the great reputation we’ve built. At the same time, we continuously look to improve our operation. Learn more about the services C&M Transport offers clients and what makes our organization stand out.

  • Specialized Equipment

    Because we work with a wide range of clients from various industries, we deliver all different types of cargo. Our specialized equipment services are intended to properly handle the variety of freight to ensure it reaches its destination safely. In addition, C&M Transport uses multiple types of equipment, such as sprinter vans, large straight trucks, and tractor-trailers up to 53 feet. All straight trucks and tractor-trailers are air-ride equipped.

  • Full Truckload

    Clients seeking a more cost-effective shipping option should consider the full truckload services from C&M Transport. This advanced shipping method involves one truck responsible for a single client’s shipment of goods from one point to another. C&M Transport offers clients door-to-door single and team transit for all truckload services to better serve a customer’s needs.
