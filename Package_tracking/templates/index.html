AIR CHARTER SERVICES

Air charter service is another name for air freight. It involves the transportation of goods by way of a cargo plane. It is a useful shipping method for oversized, potentially hazardous, or valuable items. Air charter handles about 35% of global trade by value every year. Transporting goods by cargo plane is also beneficial during seasonal peaks and when urgent delivery is needed.

AIR CHARTER SERVICES

Depending on the type of freight and where it’s headed, ground transport can be a challenge. In these instances, alternative shipping methods are required. With access to dedicated cargo planes, C&M Transport is pleased to offer clients air charter services.
Using our 3PL logistics expertise, our team coordinates all facets of the air charter transportation process. Our air charter services involve:

  • Delivering the cargo to the airport in a safe and timely fashion
  • Inspecting the cargo before it’s loaded to ensure items are still in their original conditions
  • Overseeing the loading process, making certain all cargo is properly secured.
  • Arriving at the corresponding airport before planes touch down.
  • Verifying the cargo is correct and unloaded safely from the planes onto our vehicles.
  • Safely delivering the freight to the correct location on time.
