WHAT IS AIR CHARTER SERVICE?

Air charter service is another name for air freight. It involves the transportation of goods by way of a cargo plane. It is a useful shipping method for oversized, potentially hazardous, or valuable items. Air charter handles about 35% of global trade by value every year. Transporting goods by cargo plane is also beneficial during seasonal peaks and when urgent delivery is needed.

C&M Transport has access to dedicated cargo aircraft and we maintain close relationships with operators of all plane sizes.   • Unloading at the destination

BENEFITS OF USING CARGO PLANES

There are many methods businesses can use to get their goods to customers, and you should take the time to make the most informed choice. While each can offer advantages, the following details show why air charter services may be the ideal method for your company:

Speed of Transportation

Undoubtedly, the biggest advantage of air charter solutions is the speed at which your cargo will arrive at its intended location. This factor is especially important for items that require long-distance travel. While unpredictable weather can hinder travel slightly by causing delays, air freight is still the fastest shipping method available. This can be crucial for companies transporting time-sensitive cargo, such as perishable items.

Security

An airport is one of the most closely monitored and secure facilities you can find. When choosing to transport your goods by way of air freight, you may find these strict regulations and security measures are beneficial from a customer service standpoint. From the moment your cargo is escorted onto an airfield, the risk of theft is drastically reduced. Companies that are transporting highly valuable content are likely to appreciate this feature.

Network of Destinations

Even the most secluded locations can be reached by air charter services. Planes can fly above congested roads, enabling them to reach challenging destinations with relative ease. Additionally, air cargo coordinators can provide links between multiple locations, allowing cargo to move quickly and frequently.

Cost-Effective Savings

Thanks to the speed of transporting cargo by air, the need for warehousing is significantly reduced. This allows companies to save on storage costs. Also, because cargo is generally shipped in minimal packaging to help reduce weight, companies save money on containers and materials. These cost-effective benefits can enable businesses to allocate funds to other aspects of the company.
